# Quick Start Guide

## Setup Instructions

1. **Install Flutter** (if not already installed)
   - Visit https://flutter.dev/docs/get-started/install
   - Follow the installation guide for your platform

2. **Verify Flutter Installation**
   ```bash
   flutter doctor
   ```

3. **Get Dependencies**
   ```bash
   flutter pub get
   ```

4. **Run the App**
   ```bash
   flutter run
   ```

## First Time Setup

1. **Set Your Monthly Budget**
   - Open the app
   - Go to Statistics (analytics icon)
   - Click "Set Monthly Budget"
   - Enter your monthly budget amount

2. **Start Tracking**
   - Tap "Add Transaction" or use the floating action button
   - Add your first income or expense
   - Watch your tree grow!

## Tips for Daily Use

- **Visit Daily**: Open the app every day to build your streak
- **Track Everything**: Add all your expenses to get accurate insights
- **Watch Your Tree**: The tree's health reflects your savings - keep it thriving!
- **Check Stats**: Review your spending patterns weekly

## Understanding Your Tree

- **Healthy Tree (Green & Bushy)**: You have good savings (3x your monthly budget)
- **Moderate Tree (Some Leaves)**: You have moderate savings
- **Withered Tree (Bare)**: Low or no savings - time to save more!

## Features to Explore

- **Home Screen**: View your tree and recent transactions
- **Add Transaction**: Track income and expenses
- **Statistics**: View charts, category breakdown, and achievements
- **Streaks**: Build a daily visit streak
- **Achievements**: Unlock achievements for milestones
